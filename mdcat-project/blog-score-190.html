How to Score 190+ in MDCAT 2025

10 min read

Scoring 190+ in MDCAT is achievable with the right strategy. Here's what top scorers do differently.

1. Master High-Yield Topics First

Don't study everything equally. Focus on chapters that carry the most marks — Biology (Genetics, Cell Biology), Chemistry (Organic), Physics (Mechanics).

2. Daily MCQ Practice

Solve at least 100 MCQs daily. This builds speed and accuracy. Use chapter-wise MCQ banks and track your weak areas.

3. Revision Strategy

  • Revise each chapter within 3 days of studying it
  • Use flashcards for definitions and formulas
  • Do a full syllabus revision in the last 2 weeks

4. Time Management in Exam

You have 3.5 hours for 200 MCQs — that's about 63 seconds per question. Skip difficult questions and come back to them. Never spend more than 90 seconds on one question.

5. Exam Day Tips

  • Sleep 8 hours the night before
  • Eat a light meal before the exam
  • Read every option before selecting
  • Don't change answers unless you're sure
