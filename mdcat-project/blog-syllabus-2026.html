MDCAT Syllabus 2026 — Complete Breakdown

8 min read 2025

The MDCAT 2026 syllabus is based on the PMC (Pakistan Medical Commission) curriculum covering four main subjects: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and English.

Biology (58% weightage)

Biology carries the highest weightage in MDCAT. Key chapters include Cell Biology, Genetics, Ecology, Human Physiology, Reproduction, and Biotechnology. Focus on diagrams and conceptual understanding.

  • Cell Structure & Function
  • Biological Molecules
  • Enzymes
  • Bioenergetics
  • Nutrition, Gaseous Exchange, Transport
  • Homeostasis, Coordination & Control
  • Reproduction, Genetics & Evolution
  • Biotechnology & Ecology

Chemistry (22% weightage)

Chemistry includes Organic, Inorganic and Physical Chemistry. Reactions, mechanisms and numerical problems are frequently tested.

  • Atomic Structure, Chemical Bonding
  • Stoichiometry, Gases
  • Thermodynamics, Electrochemistry
  • Organic Chemistry — Hydrocarbons, Functional Groups

Physics (12% weightage)

Physics focuses on conceptual understanding and numerical problems. Mechanics, Waves, Electromagnetism and Modern Physics are key areas.

English (8% weightage)

English tests vocabulary, grammar, sentence correction and reading comprehension. Focus on synonyms, antonyms and sentence structure.
