3-Month MDCAT Study Plan — Week by Week

7 min read

A structured 3-month plan is the most effective way to cover the entire MDCAT syllabus with time for revision and practice.

Month 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

  • Week 1–2: Biology — Cell Biology, Biological Molecules, Enzymes
  • Week 3: Chemistry — Atomic Structure, Chemical Bonding, Stoichiometry
  • Week 4: Physics — Measurements, Mechanics (Kinematics, Dynamics)

Month 2 — Core Syllabus (Weeks 5–8)

  • Week 5–6: Biology — Bioenergetics, Nutrition, Gaseous Exchange, Transport
  • Week 7: Chemistry — Thermodynamics, Electrochemistry, Organic Chemistry
  • Week 8: Physics — Waves, Thermodynamics, Electromagnetism + English Vocabulary

Month 3 — Revision & Practice (Weeks 9–12)

  • Week 9–10: Biology — Homeostasis, Genetics, Evolution, Biotechnology
  • Week 11: Full Chemistry + Physics revision
  • Week 12: Past papers daily + weak area revision

Daily Schedule

  • Morning (3 hrs): New chapter study
  • Afternoon (2 hrs): MCQ practice
  • Evening (1 hr): Revision of previous day
