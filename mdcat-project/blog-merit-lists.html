Punjab Merit Lists 2008–2025 — All Colleges

5 min read

Understanding merit trends helps you set a realistic target score. Here's a summary of closing merits for top Punjab medical colleges.

King Edward Medical University (KEMU)

  • 2024 Closing Merit: ~91.5%
  • 2023 Closing Merit: ~91.2%
  • 2022 Closing Merit: ~90.8%

Allama Iqbal Medical College (AIMC)

  • 2024 Closing Merit: ~90.8%
  • 2023 Closing Merit: ~90.5%

Fatima Jinnah Medical University (FJMU)

  • 2024 Closing Merit: ~90.2%
  • 2023 Closing Merit: ~89.9%

Rawalpindi Medical University (RMU)

  • 2024 Closing Merit: ~89.5%
  • 2023 Closing Merit: ~89.1%

How Aggregate is Calculated

Punjab aggregate = FSc Marks (40%) + Matric Marks (10%) + MDCAT Score (50%)

To get into KEMU, you need approximately 185+ in MDCAT with 90%+ in FSc.
