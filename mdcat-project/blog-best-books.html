Best Books for MDCAT 2025 — Subject Wise

6 min read

Choosing the right books is crucial for MDCAT preparation. Here are the top recommended books for each subject.

Biology

  • Kips Biology — Best for concept building and MCQ practice
  • Dogar Publishers Biology — Good for past paper MCQs
  • FSc Textbook (Punjab) — Essential base for all topics

Chemistry

  • Kips Chemistry — Comprehensive coverage with solved examples
  • Zayan Publishers Chemistry — Excellent for organic chemistry
  • FSc Textbook — Must read for all reactions and definitions

Physics

  • Kips Physics — Best for numericals and conceptual MCQs
  • Insight Physics — Good for advanced practice
  • FSc Textbook — Foundation for all topics

English

  • Dogar English Vocabulary — High-frequency word lists
  • Kips English — Grammar and comprehension practice

Our Recommendation

Use FSc textbooks as your base, Kips for practice, and our platform's notes for quick revision. Don't buy too many books — depth over breadth.
