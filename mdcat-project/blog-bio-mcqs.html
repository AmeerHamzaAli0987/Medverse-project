Biology Repeated MCQs — Most Important Topics

12 min read

These Biology topics have appeared repeatedly in MDCAT over the years. Master these and you'll secure most of your Biology marks.

Cell Biology (High Frequency)

  • Structure and function of cell organelles
  • Difference between prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells
  • Cell membrane structure — fluid mosaic model
  • Mitosis vs Meiosis stages and significance

Biological Molecules

  • Structure of DNA and RNA
  • Types of proteins and their functions
  • Enzyme action — lock and key, induced fit
  • Factors affecting enzyme activity

Genetics (Very High Frequency)

  • Mendel's laws — monohybrid and dihybrid crosses
  • Co-dominance, incomplete dominance
  • Sex-linked inheritance
  • DNA replication, transcription, translation

Human Physiology

  • Blood components and their functions
  • Heart structure and cardiac cycle
  • Nephron structure and urine formation
  • Nervous system — neuron structure, synapse
