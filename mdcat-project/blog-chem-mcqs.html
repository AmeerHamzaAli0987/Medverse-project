Chemistry Repeated MCQs — Organic & Inorganic

11 min read

Chemistry MCQs in MDCAT focus heavily on Organic reactions, Stoichiometry and Electrochemistry. Here are the most repeated topics.

Stoichiometry (Very High Frequency)

  • Mole concept and Avogadro's number
  • Limiting reagent problems
  • Percentage yield calculations
  • Empirical and molecular formula

Atomic Structure

  • Electronic configuration
  • Quantum numbers
  • Periodic trends — ionization energy, electronegativity

Organic Chemistry (High Frequency)

  • IUPAC nomenclature of hydrocarbons
  • Reactions of alkanes, alkenes, alkynes
  • Functional groups — alcohols, aldehydes, ketones, carboxylic acids
  • Addition, substitution, elimination reactions

Electrochemistry

  • Oxidation and reduction
  • Galvanic vs electrolytic cells
  • Faraday's laws
